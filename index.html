Get To Know Mercedes

Hello Everyone. My name is Mercedes Sye. I reside in Madison, WI. Since I was about 8 years old, when I got my first computer, I’ve been interested in technology and how it worked. When I played the games and visited websites, I always thought about how they worked and the process of people making these things to getting all of it to work. In the 5th grade, I was taking a technical education class, my class had an assignment to take apart and put back together hard drives. That’s what really got me interested in the technical field. My senior year of high school, I took a web design class. There I built my first un-named site using Dreamweaver, HTML and CSS and a little Javascript.

Ever since I learned about computers over the years, I got fascinated and more interested with how technology worked and changed over time. I decided to take the YWEB program at the YWCA in Madison WIsconsin, which taught me about making sites and apps that I can use. I love coding sites/apps, because it shows me and other people how the internet works and can be created to look like whatever comes to a persons mind. And that is truely amazing.
